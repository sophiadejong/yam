Alfonso Yordi is a Madrid-based designer working at the intersection
of design, art direction, and typography. Through his practice,
YAM Studio, he develops bold visual identities and
cultural experiences for clients worldwide.

Having worked across industries for both global brands and cultural,
initiatives he has collaborated with renowned studios including
Uncommon, Our Polite Society, Smol Studios, Köln Studio,
WAKA, Cocota Studio and Perlita-Calvario.
