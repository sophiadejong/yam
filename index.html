Animario is Madrid’s international contemporary animation film festival, with Poland as the guest country for its 2024 edition.

This year’s identity draws on wycinanki—the 19th-century Polish art of paper cutting used to depict daily life and decorate homes. From this research, the visual identity translates a traditional craft into a contemporary language, blending the rural with the fantastic, history with modernity.
